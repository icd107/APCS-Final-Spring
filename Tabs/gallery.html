Action Menu

This is where you decide how to take care of your cats! You can feed, play, or check on your cats. Beware! You only have a specific number of actions per day, so don't waste them!

Your Cat

You can name your cat and see its needs! @ represents a full circle and O represents an empty circle. You want filled happiness and energy, but empty hunger! Your cats also grow, so little Sadie here may not always be a kitten.

Feeding Your Cat

Feeding your cats is one of the actions you can do to keep your cat happy! You want to make sure to feed your little guy if their hunger is getting high!
